Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

CatScrap ("we," "us," or "our") operates the KitKit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using KitKit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Apple Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Unique Apple User ID (required)
  • Email address (if you choose to share it)
  • Full name (if you choose to share it)

2.2 Kit Creation Data

We store:

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript code you create in kits
  • Kit names, descriptions, and metadata
  • Kit creation and modification timestamps
  • Version history of your kits

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (features used, errors encountered)
  • Firebase Analytics data (crashes, performance metrics)

2.4 Subscription & Payment Data

If you subscribe to Premium:

  • Subscription status and expiration date
  • AI credit balance
  • Purchase history (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

3. Why We Collect This Data

3.1 Legal Accountability

Important: We require authentication because users create executable code. Linking kits to verified Apple IDs protects our service and users from:

  • Malicious code distribution
  • Copyright infringement
  • Illegal content
  • Scams and fraud

3.2 Service Functionality

  • Sync your kits across devices (future feature)
  • Maintain your subscription and credit balance
  • Provide personalized AI assistance
  • Enable future KitChain blockchain features

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers:

  • Location: United States (us-central1)
  • Encryption: At rest and in transit
  • Retention: Until you delete your account

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase (cloud hosting)
  • Apple App Store (payment processing)
  • OpenAI and Anthropic (AI API services - only code you submit, never personal data)
  • When required by law or court order

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access Your Data

Contact us to request a copy of your data.

6.2 Delete Your Account

Go to: Settings ‚Üí Account Management ‚Üí Delete Account

This permanently deletes:

  • Your user profile
  • All kits you've created
  • Credit balance and subscription history
  • Cannot be undone

6.3 Export Your Kits

Use the Export feature to save kits as .kit files before deleting your account.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted storage (Firebase encryption)
  • Authentication-required API access
  • Regular security audits
